Portfolio of M.S.Dhoni

Mahendra singh Dhoni MS Dhoni, the iconic former captain of the Indian cricket team, has built an impressive investment portfolio that extends far beyond the boundaries of the cricket field. "Captain Cool" Known for his shrewd decision-making abilities on the pitch, Dhoni has exhibited a similar acumen in the business world, diversifying his investments across various sectors. In this blog post, we delve into the various companies and startups that have captured the attention of this cricketing legend.

MS Dhoni's Investment Portfolio

Mahendra Singh Dhoni, affectionately known as "Captain Cool," has made strategic investments in a wide range of companies, spanning sectors such as automotive, home furnishings, aerospace, finance, and consumer goods. Some of the notable companies in his portfolio include EMotorad, HomeLane, Garuda Aerospace, Cars24, Khatabook, 7ink Brews, and Rigi.

Startup Investments

Dhoni has a keen eye for promising startups, and his investments have been instrumental in fueling their growth trajectories. One of his notable investments is in Garuda Aerospace, a drone company that specializes in unmanned aerial vehicles (UAVs) for various applications. In 2022, he invested an undisclosed amount in the company, recognizing its potential in the rapidly growing drone industry. Another startup that has caught Dhoni's attention is EMotorad, an electric vehicle startup focused on designing and manufacturing electric cycles and bikes. His investment in EMotorad aligns with his commitment to sustainable transportation solutions and demonstrates his forward-thinking approach to investments.

Sports Academies and Franchises

As an avid sports enthusiast, Dhoni has invested in sports academies and franchises. He co-owns the Indian Super League football team, Chennaiyin FC, and has also invested in Rhiti Sports, a sports management company. These investments allow him to stay connected to the world of sports and contribute to the development of talented athletes.
